Eternal battery life

Ever been in the middle of watching Game of Thrones and noticed your battery ran out? Say goodbye to those millenial mental breakdowns. With Apple computers, you obtain unlimited battery. Enjoy countless hours of meme watching, youtube streaming, and spaghetti programming without ever having to scrounge for a power outlet even once.

 Wifi anywhere

Apple computers count with cutting-edge Wifi tech that will allow you to obtain signal literally anywhere on Earth (or on Mars) with a breathetaking speed of 1 000 000 000 Yottabytes/second. Whether you're living in your mom's basement or whether you're vacationing in Mars, enjoy supersonic Wifi!

 Bluetooth compatibility

With the rise of the Internet of Things, our computers have the capability to connect via bluetooth with any of your household devices. Literally anything. Connect to your grandpa's TV from the 1940's to uprade it into a 2030 digital flat-screen Ultra HD 4K Smart TV.
